About


"Skill Sharing" is a project devised by the UJI's Student Council and developed by a group of students called "EI102716GGV".


We aim to ...

Encourage collaboration and mutual support among university students in different academic fields.

We do it ...

Through a web application in which users register and publish their offers for help or help needs by selecting one of the academic fields available for each publication and a time interval.

Who can do it?

Any person who is enrolled in any course of the UJI, whether of degree, master or doctorate.
